Frequently Asked Questions About Outdoor Adventure

What is the cost of attending Camp Shohola for a summer session, and are there any additional fees I should be aware of?
Our standard four-week summer session tuition for 2024 is $6,900 per camper, which covers all meals, lodging, and core activities. Optional activities like horseback riding, golf, and private waterskiing incur extra fees, which range from $150 to $500 depending on the activity. A $1,000 deposit is required upon registration, with the balance due by May 1st. There are no hidden fees, but families are responsible for their child's personal spending money and transportation to and from camp in Greeley, PA.
When does the 2024 summer camp session start and end, and what is the daily schedule like?
Our 2024 session runs from June 30th to July 27th. Drop-off is scheduled for the afternoon of June 30th, and pick-up is on the morning of July 27th. Campers start their day at 7:30 am with breakfast and end around 9:00 pm after evening activities. The daily schedule includes five activity periods, with options like archery, canoeing, rock climbing, arts, and sports, plus rest and free time. We provide a detailed schedule to families before opening day.
Is Camp Shohola licensed and accredited, and what safety measures are in place for outdoor adventure activities?
Yes, Camp Shohola is fully licensed by the Pennsylvania Department of Health and accredited by the American Camp Association (ACA), which means we meet rigorous national standards for safety and program quality. All adventure activities are supervised by certified instructors, and we maintain a 1:4 staff-to-camper ratio. Lifeguards are Red Cross-certified, and adventure staff are trained in CPR, first aid, and emergency procedures tailored to our Greeley, PA, location.
How does the registration process work, and how soon should we sign up to secure a spot?
Registration for Camp Shohola is done online through our website and is open from October through May or until spots are filled. Our sessions often reach capacity by early spring, so we recommend registering as soon as possible. After submitting your application and deposit, you'll receive a confirmation email and a welcome package with health forms, packing lists, and transportation info. Our staff is available by phone or email to assist with any questions throughout the process.
